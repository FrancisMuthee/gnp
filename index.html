Founder's note

"My name is Lucy Wanjiru Maruhi(Nyanjau). My career is in hospitality industry. I'm a founder and CEO of Shelter Connections & Events Organizer and Gura Nature Paradise. I believe in teamwork and building others through mentoring, training and empowering young women and youth in creating good health and wealth especially mental awareness.

Over and above that, I'm an environment conservationalist and a community growth oriented entreprenuer who connects the dots between the rural and urban: advantaged and disadvantaged: Networking to get opportunities for all in this vast, explorable world."
